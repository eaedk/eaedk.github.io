Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This application is an AI-powered customer discussion bot. It helps businesses communicate with their customers, answer questions, provide support, and automate conversations through messaging platforms such as WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

When users interact with the bot, we may collect and process:

  • Phone number and WhatsApp profile information
  • Text messages sent by the user
  • Audio messages, images, documents, or other shared media
  • Conversation history and customer support requests

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Reply to customer messages
  • Provide automated assistance using AI
  • Process requests, appointments, orders, or support tickets
  • Improve the quality of the service
  • Transfer conversations to a human agent when needed

3. Use of AI

Messages may be processed by artificial intelligence systems to understand user requests and generate relevant responses.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted technical providers required to operate the service, including WhatsApp, hosting providers, automation tools, AI providers, CRM systems, or business applications connected to the bot.

5. Data Retention

We keep user data only as long as necessary to provide the service, manage conversations, comply with business needs, or improve support quality.

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.
