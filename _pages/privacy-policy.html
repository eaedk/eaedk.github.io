Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data354 provides an AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot that helps businesses communicate with their customers, answer questions, provide support, and automate customer interactions.

1. Information We Collect

When users interact with our chatbot, we may collect and process the following information:

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Name or profile information shared through WhatsApp
  • Text messages sent to the chatbot
  • Audio messages, images, documents, or other media shared by the user
  • Conversation history and support requests

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Respond to customer messages
  • Provide automated assistance using AI
  • Process requests, appointments, orders, or support tickets
  • Improve the quality and reliability of our chatbot service
  • Transfer conversations to a human agent when needed

3. Use of AI Services

Some messages may be processed by artificial intelligence services in order to understand requests and generate relevant responses. These services are used only to provide chatbot functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell users' personal data. Data may be shared only with trusted technical service providers required to operate the chatbot, such as WhatsApp, hosting providers, automation tools, AI providers, or business systems connected to the chatbot.

5. Data Retention

We keep user data only for as long as necessary to provide the service, manage conversations, comply with business requirements, or improve support quality.

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.
